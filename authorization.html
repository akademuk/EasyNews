Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01.09.2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to FinMind Educational Application! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information. Please read this document carefully to understand how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide during registration.
  • Financial Information: Data about your income, expenses, investments, and other financial activities you enter into the app.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, and usage data (e.g., access times, pages viewed, and actions taken within the app).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To process and manage your financial data in order to offer personalized financial recommendations and analysis.
  • To Improve the App: To analyze app usage and improve its functionality and user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: To contact you regarding support, updates, and marketing offers (with your consent).

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with our service providers who assist us in operating the app, provided they are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information in response to legal requests or to comply with the law, protect rights, and ensure safety.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, and disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: The right to know what data we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal information if it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated Policy on our website and in the app. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
